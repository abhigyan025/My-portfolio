About Me

Kumar Abhigyan (born October 2010) is a young Indian author from Jharkhand, India. He writes analytical, data-driven commentary on geopolitics, economy, faith, and society, presenting the world through the lens of an Indian teenager.


The son of Naveen Kumar Sinha, Abhigyan developed an early curiosity for global affairs and cultural shifts. His writing is respectful yet critical, clear and structured, reflecting his unique worldview.


He has won numerous online debates centered around geopolitics, consistently delivering strong, well-structured arguments.
