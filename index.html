Meet Kumar Abhigyan

Kumar Abhigyan is a young author exploring the world through the lens of geopolitics, international relations, and social observation. His works analyse global structures, power shifts, and the narratives shaping modern society. Through books and essays, he aims to provide a thoughtful perspective on the evolving world. He is also an active debater with multiple online and debate wins, and a published author on Kindle.
