Dollar Dominance & De-Dollarization

The US dollar continues to dominate global trade and reserve systems.

Continue Reading ↓

Its dominance stems from liquidity, institutional trust, and deep capital markets. While discussions of de-dollarization are rising among emerging economies, systemic currency replacement requires long-term confidence and stability.

Shifts in settlement mechanisms may occur gradually rather than abruptly.
