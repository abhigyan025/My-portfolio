Meet Kumar Abhigyan

Kumar Abhigyan (born October 2010) is a young Indian author, writer, and commentator from Jharkhand, India. He writes analytical, data-driven texts reflecting his perspective on global affairs, society, politics, economics and culture. His work aims to present the thoughts of an Indian teenager in a respectful, clear and critically thoughtful manner.

Abhigyan developed a strong curiosity about international politics, faith and societal dynamics from an early age. His writing combines reflection with structured analysis, enabling readers to engage with complex issues through the lens of a thoughtful young observer.

He is particularly known for his participation in online debates focused on geopolitics and world affairs, where he has won numerous competitions by presenting well-structured arguments and persuasive reasoning.

Beyond writing and debate, Abhigyan enjoys music, motorsports, cooking, and exploring new perspectives through discussion and research.

Essays

The Illusion of Stability

Modern global stability often appears stronger than it truly is. Institutions function, diplomacy operates and economic systems interconnect nations across continents.

Yet beneath this appearance lies a far more fragile structure built upon political consensus, economic interdependence and carefully maintained narratives of order.

Understanding this fragility reveals how quickly stability can transform into uncertainty when underlying assumptions collapse.
