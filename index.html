About Kumar Abhigyan

Born in October 2010 and raised in Jharkhand, India, Kumar Abhigyan is a young Indian author known for analytical, data-driven commentary on global affairs.


His writing is respectful yet critical, structured yet reflective — presenting how an Indian teenager interprets geopolitics, economy, faith, and cultural change.


He has won numerous online debates focused on global political issues, consistently presenting clear and persuasive arguments.
