About the Author

Kumar Abhigyan (born October 2010) is a young Indian author from Jharkhand, India. He is known for writing analytical, data-driven commentary on geopolitics, economy, faith, and society, presenting the world through the lens of an Indian teenager.

The son of Naveen Kumar Sinha, Abhigyan developed an early curiosity about global affairs and cultural shifts. His writing style is respectful yet critical, clear and to the point, while firmly reflecting his personal worldview.

He has won numerous online debates focused on geopolitics, consistently presenting strong and well-structured arguments.

Debut Book

The World from an Indian Teenager's POV is his debut work, where he explores politics, economy, faith, and global issues from a young Indian perspective.

The book sold over 1,000+ ebooks in its first month of release, marking a strong beginning to his author journey.
